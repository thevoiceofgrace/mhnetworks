About Us

Who We Are

Pleroma Telecommunications is a community-focused Internet Service Provider (ISP) dedicated to providing fast and reliable internet in the most affordable way possible.

We use innovative methods to deliver premium connectivity without the costs and complications of traditional providers. We are not resellers — instead, we provide original solutions that empower communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to close the digital divide by making internet access inclusive, simple, and sustainable.

We are focused on expanding beyond townships into rural areas, where connectivity is often unreliable or unaffordable. By doing this, we aim to empower communities with access to education, communication, and economic opportunities.

Business Opportunities

At Pleroma, we believe in shared growth. That’s why we are creating opportunities for entrepreneurs through our franchising model.

This allows motivated individuals to partner with us, extend our services to their own communities, and contribute to building a connected future while creating sustainable businesses.
